Frequently asked questions

What is CVio and how does it work? +
CVio is an AI-powered CV analysis platform. You upload your CV, tell us the role you are targeting, and our AI instantly gives you a score out of 100, ATS compatibility check, keyword gaps, salary estimate, and specific steps to improve. It works for every industry, every role, and every country.
What is an ATS and why does it matter? +
ATS stands for Applicant Tracking System. It is software that most companies - including 99% of large employers - use to automatically filter CVs before a human recruiter sees them. If your CV does not pass the ATS filter, no one will ever read it. CVio checks your ATS compatibility and tells you exactly what to fix.
How many free analyses do I get? +
You get 5 free CV analyses when you sign up - no credit card needed. After that, you can subscribe to the Quarterly plan at 13.99 per month (billed every 3 months) or the Monthly plan at 17.99 per month. The Bullet Rewriter is always free with no limits.
Is my CV data private and secure? +
Yes. Your CV is processed in real time and is never stored on our servers. We do not share your data with anyone. The analysis happens instantly and the text is discarded after your results are generated. You can use CVio with complete confidence.
What file formats does CVio accept? +
CVio accepts PDF, Word (.docx), and plain text (.txt) files. For the best results we recommend uploading a Word document as it allows us to read the text most accurately. PDF files are also well supported.
Does CVio work for all industries and countries? +
Yes - CVio works for every industry (tech, finance, healthcare, marketing, legal, education, engineering, creative, and more) and every country. Our AI adapts its advice based on the role and location you specify. Whether you are applying in the UK, USA, India, Australia, or anywhere else, CVio gives you relevant, targeted advice.
What is the difference between Quarterly and Monthly plans? +
Both plans give you identical features - unlimited CV analyses, JD matching, interview prep, bullet rewriting, and the AI coach. The only difference is billing frequency. Quarterly is billed as 41.97 every 3 months (13.99/month equivalent). Monthly is billed as 17.99 each month. Quarterly saves you 12 every 3 months and is ideal if you are actively job searching.
Can I cancel my subscription at any time? +
Yes - you can cancel at any time with no penalty. For the Monthly plan, cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing month. For the Quarterly plan, cancellation takes effect at the end of your current 3-month period. You keep full access until then.
How do I make my CV pass ATS filters? +
To pass ATS: use standard section headings (Work Experience, Education, Skills), mirror keywords directly from the job description, avoid tables, columns, graphics, and headers/footers, use a clean readable font, and save as PDF or Word. CVio checks all of this automatically and tells you exactly what to fix in your ATS score.
How do I explain a gap in my CV? +
Be honest and brief. State what you did during the gap - freelancing, studying, caring for family, health recovery, or personal development. Frame it positively and move on. Employers understand career gaps - what concerns them is an unexplained gap with no context at all. The CVio AI Coach can help you write a strong personal statement that addresses your gap confidently.
How do I negotiate a higher salary? +
Research market rates on Glassdoor, LinkedIn Salary, and similar sites before any salary conversation. Always give a range rather than a single number. Let the employer make the first offer where possible. Never accept on the spot - it is always acceptable to say you need 24 hours to consider. Use CVio's salary estimator to understand your market value before every application.
Can I use CVio to change careers? +
Absolutely. CVio is one of the best tools for career changers. Use the JD Matcher to paste a job description from your target industry and see exactly how your current CV matches up. The gap analysis shows you which transferable skills to highlight and which new skills to mention. The Bullet Rewriter helps you reframe your existing experience in language that resonates with your new target industry.
How long should my CV be? +
2 pages is the standard for most professionals. Use 1 page if you have under 2 years of experience. Use 3 pages only if you are a very senior professional with 15+ years of highly relevant experience. Recruiters spend an average of 7 seconds on a CV at first glance - every line must earn its place. CVio will tell you if your CV is too long or too short for your experience level.
