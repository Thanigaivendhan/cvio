Privacy Policy

GDPR Compliant Last updated: March 2026

CVio is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect only what we need, never sell your data, and your CV content is never stored on our servers.

1. Who we are

CVio is an AI-powered CV analysis platform based in the United Kingdom. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at support@cvio.co.uk.

2. What data we collect

We collect the following personal data when you use CVio:

  • Account data: Your name and email address when you sign up
  • Usage data: Number of analyses completed, subscription status
  • Feedback data: Star ratings and feedback messages you submit voluntarily
  • Analytics data: Anonymous page visit data via Google Analytics (pages visited, time on site, country — never personal identity)
We do NOT store your CV content. When you upload a CV, it is processed in real time to generate your analysis and then immediately discarded. We never store, read, or share the text of your CV.

3. How we use your data

  • To create and manage your account
  • To track your usage and apply the correct plan limits
  • To improve CVio based on aggregate usage patterns
  • To respond to your feedback and support requests
  • To send you important account-related emails (no marketing emails without your explicit consent)

4. Legal basis for processing (GDPR)

If you are in the UK or European Union, we process your data under the following legal bases:

  • Contract: Processing necessary to provide the CVio service you signed up for
  • Legitimate interests: Improving our service, preventing fraud, understanding usage patterns
  • Consent: For any optional communications or cookies beyond strictly necessary ones

5. Data storage and security

Your account data is stored securely in Supabase, a GDPR-compliant database hosted in West Europe (London). We use industry-standard encryption and security practices. We do not transfer your personal data outside the UK or EU.

6. How long we keep your data

We keep your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will permanently delete your personal data within 30 days. Anonymised analytics data may be retained indefinitely as it cannot be linked back to you.

7. Your rights

Under UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to or restrict the processing of your data
  • Data portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the ICO (Information Commissioner's Office)

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@cvio.co.uk and we will respond within 5 business days.

8. Third-party services

  • Supabase — Database storage (GDPR compliant, EU/UK hosting)
  • Groq API (LLaMA) — AI analysis processing. Your CV text is sent to Groq's API for processing but is not stored by them per their data processing terms
  • Netlify — Website hosting (SOC 2 Type II certified)
  • Google Analytics — Anonymous usage analytics only. No personally identifiable data is collected

9. Children

CVio is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 16. If you believe a child under 16 has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or by a prominent notice on the CVio website at least 14 days before changes take effect.

🔒 Questions about your privacy? Email us at support@cvio.co.uk — we respond within 5 business days and take every privacy enquiry seriously.
